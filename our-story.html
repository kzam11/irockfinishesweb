About Us

Our Story

A passion for stone, a commitment to craft, and 15+ years serving Melbourne and Victoria's finest homes and commercial spaces.

At iRock Finishes, we're passionate about the restoration, rejuvenation, cleaning and sealing of natural stone, unglazed tiles and concrete. We provide a personal, professional service and pride ourselves on our integrity and work ethic.

Who We Are

We are Victoria's number one stone restoration and polishing service. With over 15 years of hands-on industry experience, we have built a reputation for excellence across Melbourne and regional Victoria — from luxury residential homes to major commercial and infrastructure projects.

Our team of certified experts understands that every surface is different. We take the time to assess each job individually, recommending the most effective treatment for your specific stone, tile or concrete type.

What Drives Us

We believe that stone and marble are long-term investments. Those who invest in stone or marble do so with a view to the long life that this incredibly durable material offers. Our mission is to protect that investment — restoring surfaces to their original glory and ensuring they stay that way.

While marble is viewed as a very hard material, the truth is that it is susceptible to scratching, staining and damage, as well as absorption. That's why proper, professional maintenance matters — and why we're here.

Pro Tips

Keeping Your Floors Scratch-Free

Once we restore your surfaces, a little care goes a long way. Here are our professional tips to protect your investment between services.

  • Restrict pets to clipped toenails or use pet shoes on polished areas
  • Avoid dragging furniture across polished floors
  • Place thick cloth under furniture legs when moving items
  • Wear shoes with rubber or soft soles (avoid rough-soled footwear)
  • Never drop heavy objects on stone or marble surfaces
  • Never use acid or harsh chemical cleaners on polished surfaces
