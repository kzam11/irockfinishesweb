Marble & Stone Restoration

Is your stone or marble floor scratched and dull? Have polishing attempts failed to restore its original shine? iRock Finishes uses industrial diamond polishing systems and specialist techniques to return marble and stone surfaces to their original — or better — condition.

  • Marble benchtop and splashback restoration
  • Acid and oil stain removal
  • Industrial diamond polishing to desired finish

Safety

Non-Slip Flooring Coating

Slips are caused by a loss of traction between the floor surface and footwear. Our non-slip coating treatment works by adding microscopic particles to the surface, creating an ultra-fine traction layer that dramatically improves safety — without altering the appearance of your floors.

Suitable for tile, marble, stone, concrete and brick surfaces. The process is quick, inexpensive and causes minimal business disruption — with no unpleasant odours or tackiness after application.

  • Microscopic particle traction treatment
  • Works on tile, marble, stone, concrete and brick
  • Dramatically improves floor safety
  • Enhances appearance and long-term durability
  • Quick application — minimal disruption to business
  • No odours or tackiness after treatment
  • Residential and commercial applications
  • Melbourne and regional Victoria
