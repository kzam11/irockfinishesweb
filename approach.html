How We Work

Our Approach

Four pillars that define everything we do — quality, superior materials, certified expertise, and the experience to back it all up.

Our Philosophy

What Sets Us Apart

01

Quality — Restoring Your Investment

Scratched, dulled or damaged stone and marble deserves professional attention — not DIY polishes that can make things worse. We use innovative machinery and proven systems to restore shine, smoothness and beauty to surfaces of all types.

Our work addresses everything from light surface scratches to deep staining, acid burns and efflorescence. We assess the condition and recommend the right treatment, never over-promising or under-delivering.

02

Superior Materials — Protecting What Matters

The products we use make all the difference. We work exclusively with premium-grade sealers and restoration compounds that are proven to perform over the long term. Our complete sealer range is backed by a 15-year warranty — because we believe in standing behind our work.

We also advise clients on preventing degradation through regular maintenance. Neglect can turn a simple clean into a full restoration — the right products, applied correctly, save you time and money in the long run.

03

Certified Experts — Skilled, Trained Professionals

Stone restoration is a specialist trade. Our team is trained and certified in the techniques required to treat every surface type safely and effectively — from rare natural marble and limestone to polished concrete and unglazed tiles.

We help our clients plan effective maintenance schedules, understanding that different properties experience varying levels of wear. And we're available by phone to answer questions even after the job is done.

04

Wealth of Experience — A Track Record You Can Trust

With over 15 years in the industry, we've seen every surface type and condition imaginable. From luxury residential apartments and 5-star hotels to major infrastructure projects like the Melbourne Metro Tunnel — our experience means you get the right solution the first time.

Our trusted client list includes ProBuild, Mirvac, Lendlease, Hickory, Hacer Group and many more. When Melbourne's biggest builders trust us with their most prominent projects, that's the quality of work you can expect for yours.

Step by Step

Our Restoration Process

Every project follows our proven four-step methodology — from initial assessment through to sealed, protected surfaces.

1

Strip & Clean

Furniture clearance and thorough removal of dirt, grime, grease, oils and surface contaminants. A clean start is essential.

2

Hone & Polish

Industrial diamond polishing system removes scratches, stains, acid burns and surface damage. Clients can stop here for a matte finish.

3

Finish & Buff

Diamond polishing tailored to the desired shine level. Crystallisation enhances natural colour and surface lustre.

4

Seal & Protect

Premium sealer application provides long-term protection against staining and damage. Backed by our 15-year warranty.

15-Year Warranty

We Back Our Work

A 15-year warranty applies to our entire range of sealers. We're confident in the quality of our products and the precision of our application — and we're prepared to put that in writing. When you invest in iRock Finishes, you're investing in genuine long-term protection.
