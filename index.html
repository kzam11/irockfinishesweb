How It Works

Our Restoration Process

A proven four-step methodology delivering consistent, lasting results on every project.

1

Strip & Clean

Furniture clearance, removal of built-up dirt, grease, oils and surface contaminants.

2

Hone & Polish

Industrial diamond polishing system removes scratches, stains, acid burns and surface damage.

3

Finish & Buff

Polish to desired finish — matte, satin or high-gloss — tailored to the stone type and client preference.

4

Seal & Protect

Premium sealer application for long-term protection. Backed by our 15-year warranty.
